Most systems don't fail because of a single decision. They fail when pressure reveals what they were never designed to handle.

Critical Path is a podcast and writing project about the systems shaping the modern world, and what happens when those systems are put under pressure.

Technology, policy (not politics), infrastructure, and the people who operate them.

Hosts

  • David Trumpey

    David Trumpey is a systems-minded operator and product builder focused on how complex systems behave under pressure.

    His work spans strategy, design, product, and execution, shaping a practical understanding of how work actually happens, where decisions break down, and why systems fail in ways no one planned for.

    He now focuses on applying AI to real workflows, using it to redesign how work gets done. Moving from experimentation to implementation requires structure, not prompts, and clear thinking, not just capability.

    Critical Path reflects that lens. Systems, constraints, and the people responsible for making them hold when it matters.
